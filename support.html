Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to common investor questions.
Interest is calculated on a simple interest basis using your principal, the agreed annual rate, and your tenure. For example: ₦5,000,000 at 18.5% for 12 months = ₦925,000 total interest.
You may request early liquidation at any time via the My Investments page. Early liquidation before the agreed tenure may attract a penalty of up to 10% of accrued interest. Your full principal is always returned.
Go to My Investments and click "Add Investment". Submit your request with the amount, tenure, and type. Your relationship manager will contact you within 24 hours to confirm and provide payment instructions.
At maturity, payouts are processed within 2 business days to your linked bank account. For early liquidation, processing takes 3–5 business days subject to approval.
Bank account changes must be verified by your relationship manager for security. Please contact us with a valid ID and recent bank statement.
STI Assets Management operates under SEC Nigeria regulatory oversight. Your investments are backed by the assets under our management. Please refer to your investment agreement for full terms.
